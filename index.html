Bronx Community District 11 Has Only One Greenthumb Community Garden

By Rachael Robertson

11 November 2021

New York City has more than 500 Greenthumb Community Gardens. Brooklyn hosts the most with 226 while Staten Island only has four. The Bronx has more than 100, but the distribution within the borough is not equitable. Areas in the south and west Bronx have many gardens per district while the east and north Bronx have far fewer.

Chart depicting the number of Greenthumb Community Gardens per borough.

Community District 11 only has one Greenthumb Community Garden. Christian Amato, 33, who serves on the transportation committee for Bronx Community Board 11 and lives in the area, thinks the area needs more green spaces.

“That need is augmented in other ways,” said Amato. “NYCHA complexes have community gardens too. Plus, we’re so close to the New York Botanical Garden.”

Chart breaking down Bronx Greenthumb Community Gardens per Community District.

New Yorkers find creative ways to experience nature, but more gardens helps people more easily access green space.
